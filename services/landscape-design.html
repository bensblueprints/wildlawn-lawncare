Our Design Process

Consultation

We visit your property, listen to your vision, assess the space, and discuss your budget and timeline. This initial consultation is completely free.

Design Concept

Our team creates a detailed design plan with plant selections, hardscape layouts, and visual mockups so you can see your new landscape before we begin.

Proposal & Approval

We present a detailed proposal with itemized pricing. You review, we revise as needed, and once approved, we schedule your installation.

Installation

Our experienced crew brings the design to life. We handle everything from site prep to planting, hardscape installation, and final cleanup.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does landscape design cost?

Projects range widely based on scope. Small bed renovations can start around $500, while full property redesigns typically range from $3,000-$15,000+. We work with your budget to maximize impact.

How long does a typical project take?

Design phase is usually 1-2 weeks. Installation depends on scope: small projects take 1-3 days, while larger designs can take 1-2 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before starting.

Do you offer a warranty on plants?

Yes. We provide a 1-year warranty on all plant material installed by our team, provided the recommended care instructions are followed.

Can you work with my existing landscape?

Absolutely. Many clients want to enhance rather than replace. We'll assess what's working, what can stay, and how to integrate new elements seamlessly.
