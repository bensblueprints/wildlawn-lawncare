Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the design process take from start to finish?

The timeline depends on project scope. A typical residential landscape design takes 2-3 weeks from consultation to final plan. Installation can range from a few days for simple plantings to 2-4 weeks for large projects with hardscape, drainage, and extensive planting. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What plants work best in the Metro Atlanta area?

Metro Atlanta sits in USDA zones 7b and 8a, giving us a wide range of options. We love native azaleas, loropetalum, boxwood, knock-out roses, hydrangeas, crepe myrtles, and ornamental grasses like muhly grass. We select plants based on your specific sun/shade conditions, soil type, and desired maintenance level.

Do you handle permits for hardscape and construction?

Yes. For projects that require permits — such as retaining walls over a certain height, significant grading, or structures — we handle the permitting process with your local municipality. We're familiar with requirements in Cobb, Cherokee, and Fulton counties.

Do you offer maintenance after the installation is complete?

Absolutely. We offer ongoing maintenance plans that include seasonal pruning, mulch refresh, fertilization, weed control, and irrigation management. We also provide a detailed care guide at project completion so you know exactly what your new landscape needs to thrive.
