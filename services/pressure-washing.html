Frequently Asked Questions

Will pressure washing damage my siding?

No. We use a soft-wash technique for all siding types, which uses low pressure combined with specialized cleaning solutions. It's safe for vinyl, brick, stucco, Hardie board, and painted surfaces.

How often should I pressure wash my home?

In Metro Atlanta's humid climate, we recommend annual house washing and driveway cleaning. Decks and fences may need cleaning every 1-2 years depending on sun exposure and shade.

Do I need to be home during the service?

Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the areas being cleaned and a working outdoor water spigot, we can complete the work while you're away.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for plants and pets?

Yes. We use biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions and take care to pre-wet and protect landscaping around work areas.
