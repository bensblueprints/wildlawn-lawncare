FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

During the growing season (April through October), most Atlanta lawns need weekly mowing. Bermuda and Zoysia grow aggressively in summer heat and may need mowing every 5-7 days. Fescue lawns grow fastest in spring and fall. In winter, bi-weekly or monthly visits are typically sufficient.

It depends on your grass type. Bermuda thrives at 1-2 inches, Zoysia at 1.5-2.5 inches, and Fescue at 3-4 inches. We never remove more than one-third of the blade height per mowing, which keeps your lawn healthy and reduces stress. We will set the height specifically for your grass type.

Every visit includes four services: professional mowing with commercial equipment, string trimming around all obstacles and bed edges, blade-cut edging along driveways, sidewalks, and curbs, and a thorough blowing of all hard surfaces. We leave your property looking manicured and clean every time.

If heavy rain prevents us from mowing on your scheduled day, we will reschedule within 1-2 business days at no extra charge. Light rain does not usually stop us — our commercial mowers can handle damp grass. We will always communicate with you if there is a schedule change.
