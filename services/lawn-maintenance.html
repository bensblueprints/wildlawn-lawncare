Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed in Atlanta?

During the growing season (April through October), we recommend weekly mowing for warm-season grasses like Bermuda and Zoysia. Fescue lawns may need weekly cuts in spring and fall. We'll tailor a schedule that keeps your lawn at the ideal height.

Do you provide service year-round?

Yes. While mowing frequency decreases in winter, we offer year-round maintenance plans that include leaf removal, edging, and pre-season prep so your lawn is always cared for.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater Metro Atlanta area including Marietta, Kennesaw, Roswell, Alpharetta, Woodstock, Acworth, Canton, Smyrna, and Vinings. Contact us to confirm service availability at your address.

Can I cancel or skip a service visit?

Absolutely. We understand plans change. Just give us 24 hours notice and we'll adjust your schedule. No cancellation fees for occasional skips on recurring plans.
