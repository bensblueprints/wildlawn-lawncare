How does the "First Service Free" offer work?

When you sign a 1-year annual lawn care agreement with Wild Lawn, your very first service visit is completely free — no strings attached. We'll come out, perform a full service (mowing, edging, trimming, blowing), and you pay nothing for that first visit. Your regular billing begins with the second visit.

What happens if I need to cancel the annual agreement?

We understand circumstances change. If you need to cancel, we ask for 30 days written notice. If you cancel within the first 90 days, you'll simply be billed for the free service you received at the standard per-visit rate. After 90 days, you can cancel with no additional charges.

Annual plan members save an average of 15% compared to our standard per-service pricing. For a typical Metro Atlanta lawn receiving weekly service, that works out to $500-$800+ in savings per year — plus the value of your free first service. The exact savings depend on your property size and service frequency.
