Spring & Fall Yard Cleanup Done Right

A clean slate for your lawn every season.

Metro Atlanta's beautiful hardwood trees — oaks, maples, sweetgums, and poplars — put on a spectacular show every autumn, but they also leave behind mountains of leaves. Without timely removal, those leaves mat down over your lawn, suffocate grass, and create conditions for fungal disease. Wild Lawn's fall cleanup service tackles leaf removal efficiently, protecting your turf through the dormant winter months.

In spring, the focus shifts to preparing your yard for the growing season ahead. We clear away winter debris, cut back dead perennial growth, edge beds, and get everything ready for fresh mulch and new growth. For homeowners in Roswell, Alpharetta, and Canton, spring cleanup is the foundation for a lawn that looks great all summer long.

Our seasonal cleanup crews come equipped with commercial blowers, rakes, tarps, and hauling trailers. We don't just push leaves around — we remove them from your property completely. Properties in Acworth and Kennesaw with heavily wooded lots benefit especially from our thorough approach, which includes clearing leaves from beds, gutters, and hardscape areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I schedule fall cleanup in Atlanta?

Most leaves drop between late October and mid-December in Metro Atlanta. We recommend scheduling your fall cleanup for late November or early December to catch the bulk of the leaf drop. For heavily wooded properties, a second cleanup in January may be helpful.

What does spring cleanup include?

Spring cleanup includes removing winter debris, cutting back dead perennials and ornamental grasses, cleaning and edging landscape beds, raking out matted leaves, and a general property assessment to identify any winter damage that needs attention.

Do you haul away all the debris?

Yes. All leaves, branches, and yard debris are removed from your property as part of our cleanup service. We don't leave bags at the curb — everything goes with us.

Can I combine cleanup with other services?

Absolutely. Many clients in Marietta and Woodstock combine seasonal cleanup with mulch installation, shrub pruning, or fertilization. Bundling services saves you money and gets your yard fully prepared in one visit.
